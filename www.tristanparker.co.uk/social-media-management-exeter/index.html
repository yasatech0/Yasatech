Do I Need A Social Media Manager?

If you post infrequently or dont use social media for business growth, then you may be happy to manage your activity yourself. However, If you want to stay current, post regularly on multiple channels and produce regular engaging, original content, then a social media manager can alleviate the strain and free up your valuable time. Not to mention, an experience social media manager Is likely to get you results quicker and more effectively, thus saving you money and improving your ROI.

Boosted Posts VS Facebook Ads Whats the Difference?

A boosted post is simply paying to have more people view your post, no matter whether these people are interested in your offering or not. There is very little control or targeting options available and can often be dead money. Meanwhile, Facebook advertisement gives you control and allows you to tailor the audience perimeters. Therefore, the people that see your advert will be the people who are most likely to use/buy/benefit from it. Thus, increasing interaction and generating genuine interest, resulting in a much better ROI.
